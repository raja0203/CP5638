CP1406 - Assignment 2 - Project Plan - Townsville Community Music Centre

Name: SPRR

Goals

Townsville Community Music Centre, provides concerts and workshops throughout the year across different genres and contemporary music. With an overwhelming response from touring artist,locally based professional and emerged artists TCMC wants to redesign their website which can enable them to increase Membership and organising concerts in Townsville.

Membership
  1. Recruiting new members
  2. To increase engagement of members for volounterring, organising and promiting concerts.
Concerts
  1. Increasing ticket sales for general public
  2. Organising concert for talented performers
  3. Encouraging performers with promotion of concert by all means such as photo shoots, media releases

Success Evaluation

With an aim to redesign website for organising concert with distinctive theme, Townsville Community Music Centre website has to be creative and backed by good strategy at the time of designing the site. This website will be designed in such a way it ensures an easy access to all its content with clear navigation links besides making it easy for recruiting new members and increasing ticket sales for the target audience.

Target Audience

Description
With an intention to provide opportunities for a wide range of styles and genres that supports our theme "Specialise in Diversity" The idea of redesigning TCMC was primarily intended to reach out to all sets of member across different parts of Townsville
Primary Users
- General people with specialised genre taste
Secondary Users
- Children involved with parents and family members.
User Characteristics
- Age / Gender: 15+ / Both male and female
- Education: Secondary school or higher
- Computer and web experience: Online Ticket booking
